About the MCU

The MCU is an American media franchise which focuses on the series of superhero films based on the Marvel comic books. The franchise made its debut back in 2008 with the superhero character Ironman. The movie was a starting point for the MCU franchise only to find out ten years later to become a huge global success. Within ten years the MCU has grossed over $17.5 billion at the global box office.
So far the MCU has made a total of 20 movies, and more to come. Some of these movies include the famous titles such as;

  • Iron Man (2008)
  • Marvel's Avengers (2012)
  • Captain America: Winter Soldier (2014)
  • Captain America: Cival War (2016)
  • Doctor Strange (2016)
  • Spider-Man: Homecoming (2017)
  • Avengers: Infinity War (2018)
